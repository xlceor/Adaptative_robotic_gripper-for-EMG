# Base v1

## Purpose
Base of the four-bar linkage. It serves as a support and for storing the electronics, including the servo.

## Geometry
The shape was designed in base of the structure of the servo, to link easily and mantain streght.

## Material
PLA or PETG, depending on testing

## Comments of the version
As readed before, the shape was modeled in base of the servomotor, a MG996R, to fit well on the servo and let the servo output at the base level of the piece. The piece was designed for use gears.
## Next Steps
