# Base v3.5

## Purpose
Base of the four-bar linkage. It serves as a support and for storing the electronics, including the servos, buck converter, and MCU.

## Geometry
The shape was designed in base of the structure of 2 servos tandem-mounted and the module Xl4016, with sections on the sides to store MCU, power supply board, and the batteries

## Material
PLA for testing
