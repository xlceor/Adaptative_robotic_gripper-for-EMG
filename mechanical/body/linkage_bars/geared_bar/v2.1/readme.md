# Geared bars v2.1

## Purpose
principal bars of the mechanism. Translates the servo output to the open/closing mechaism of the gripper. 

## Geometry
The design consist on 2 bars, one per each side, and a gear designed for the servo output. The A bar have a compound gear, lower for reduction(2:1 of the servo output) and upper for transmision(1:1).

## Material
PLA or PETG, depending on testing

## Comments of the version
The real-life testing shows that the shape are limiting the maximum open/closing of the gripper, specially on opening. So, in V2 the shape was designed to provide the maximum grade of freedom. This version solves minor problems, like the width of the screw-holes.
