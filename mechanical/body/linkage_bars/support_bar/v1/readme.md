# Support bar v1

## Purpose
Secondary bar of the two support bars. Gives stability and lets the mechanism acts as a specific way, depending on center-to-center distance.

## Geometry
The shape was minimalist, adjusting the witdh in base on srew-holes

## Material
PLA or PETG, depending on testing

## Comments of the version
The center-to-center distance was increased, to add more freedom to the entire mechanism. Plus, the results of real life testing was that, although works, the simple shape limits the mechanism to open/close to the maximum, especially on closing.
## Next Steps
Adjust the design to add more freedom.
