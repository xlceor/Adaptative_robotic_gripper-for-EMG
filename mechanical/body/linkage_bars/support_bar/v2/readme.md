# Support bar v2

## Purpose
Secondary bar of the two support bars. Gives stability and lets the mechanism acts as a specific way, depending on center-to-center distance.

## Geometry
The shape was designed to give full range of motion.

## Material
PLA or PETG, depending on testing

## Comments of the version
Unlike the previous models, this version consists of two pieces, each one designed to provide the best movement range on closing the gripper.
