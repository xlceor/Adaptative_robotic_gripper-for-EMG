# Cover v3

## Purpose
Close the design and keep all internal components hidden, improving the aesthetics.

## Geometry
The shape was designed based on the body's design. It was divided into three parts, each corresponding to a section of the body. This allows for quicker access to a specific component if needed, without having to open the entire device.

## Material
PLA for testing

## Comments of the version
Due to time reasons, the body itself was not updated for the mounting points. In this version self-tapping screws was used to close the device without reprinting all the design. Also, some slots was added for better ventilation on components as the buck, and the servos, but also to keep acces to some components without removing the case, like the switch and the alimentation of ESP32.
