# TPU Finger Prototype v1.1

## Purpose
TPU finger of adaptable soft-robotic gripper. 

## Geometry
Right triangle base with parallel internal bending lines to allow controlled deformation.

## Material
TPU 95A

## Comments of the version
According to observations, the finger proved to be quite rigid, primarily due to the support lines parallel to the longer leg of the triangle, which is the area where the object is gripped. In this version, the orientation, number, and thickness of the parallel lines were modified, tilting them at a 50-degree angle to the base, allowing for controlled deformation. However, this version still exhibits considerable rigidity, causing more fragile objects to tend to break or deform when the claw closes. Also, the upper tip can be somewhat sharp and still represents wasted material.

## Next Steps
Adjust the thickness of the internal walls to reduce rigidity, and round the upper tip.
