# TPU Finger Prototype v1.1

## Purpose
TPU finger of adaptable soft-robotic gripper. 

## Geometry
Right triangle base with parallel internal bending lines to allow controlled deformation.

## Material
TPU 95A

## Comments of the version
Based on the previous observations, the stiffness of the finger was reduced by making the internal walls thinner. Also, the upper tip was rounded, reducing sharpness and the filament used. Even so, the wall used to grip objects was flat, reducing gripping ability. 
## Next Steps
Give the external wall texture to add gripping capacity
